The Contacts overview provides a global directory of all contacts across every customer in ARMS. Unlike the contacts tab on a customer detail page, this view is not scoped to a single customer and allows you to search and filter across the entire contact database.

<Callout kind="info">
  This page is accessible to users with the **Admin**, **Commercial**, **Accounting**, **Fleet Manager**, or **Read-Only** role.
</Callout>

## Contact list

The contact list displays all contacts in a paginated table with the following columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | Full name of the contact |
| **Email** | Contact email address |
| **Phone** | Contact phone number |
| **Function / Role** | The contact's job title or role within their organization |
| **Parent customer** | The customer this contact belongs to (linked to the customer record) |
| **Company** | The company name associated with the contact |

## Search and filter

Use the search bar to find contacts by name, email, or phone number. You can also filter the list by parent customer or company to narrow down results.

## Navigating to a customer

Click the **Parent customer** link on any contact row to navigate directly to that customer's detail page. From there, you can view all contacts associated with that specific customer.
