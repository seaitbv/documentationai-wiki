---
title: "Locations"
description: "Manage pick-up locations for trailers and contracts in ARMS."
---

Locations represent the physical pick-up points where trailers are stationed. Each location belongs to a specific company (Atrac or Urbain) and is used throughout the system in fleet and contract forms.

<Callout kind="alert">
  Location management is restricted to users with the **Admin** role.
</Callout>

## Location list

The location list displays all registered pick-up locations. Use the **company filter** dropdown at the top of the page to show locations for a specific company.

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | The location's display name |
| **Address** | Physical address of the location |
| **Company** | The company this location belongs to (Atrac or Urbain) |

## Creating a location

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Locations page" icon="map-pin" titleType="p">
    Navigate to **Administration > Locations** in the sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Create" icon="plus" titleType="p">
    Click the **Create** button to open the new location form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the location details" icon="text-cursor" titleType="p">
    Enter the location name, address, and select the company this location belongs to.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the location" icon="save" titleType="p">
    Click **Save** to create the location.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a location

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a location" icon="mouse-pointer" titleType="p">
    Click a location row to open its detail view.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Edit" icon="edit" titleType="p">
    Click the **Edit** button to make the fields editable.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update the desired fields" icon="text-cursor" titleType="p">
    Modify the location name, address, or company assignment as needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes" icon="save" titleType="p">
    Click **Save** to apply your changes. Click **Cancel** to discard them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Locations appear as selectable options in trailer forms (pick-up location field) and in contract forms. Changing a location's details updates how it displays across the system, but does not affect historical records.
</Callout>
