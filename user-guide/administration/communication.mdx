---
title: "Communication"
description: "Track and manage all emails sent from ARMS, including offer and contract communications."
---

The Communication page provides a centralized log of all emails sent from ARMS. This includes emails triggered from offers and contracts, giving you a complete audit trail of outbound communications.

<Callout kind="info">
  The Communication page is accessible to users with the **Admin**, **Commercial**, **Accounting**, or **Read Only** role.
</Callout>

## Filter options

Use the filters at the top of the page to narrow down the communication log:

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Entity type** | Offer, Contract |
| **Sent status** | Draft, Sent |
| **Date range** | Start and end date picker |

## Communication log columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Date** | The date the email was created or sent |
| **Entity type** | Whether the email relates to an Offer or Contract |
| **Entity ID** | The linked offer or contract identifier (click to navigate to the record) |
| **Recipients** | Email addresses of all recipients |
| **Subject** | The email subject line |
| **Status** | Badge indicating **Draft** or **Sent** |

## Mark as sent

Emails that were composed but not yet dispatched through ARMS appear with a **Draft** status. You can manually mark these as sent by selecting the email and clicking **Mark as sent**.

<Callout kind="tip">
  The "Mark as sent" action is useful when you sent an email outside of ARMS (for example, directly from your email client) and want to update the communication log to reflect the correct status.
</Callout>
