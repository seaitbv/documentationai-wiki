---
title: "Companies"
description: "Manage company entities in ARMS, including company details, branding, and bank account information."
---

ARMS supports two separate company entities: **Atrac** and **Urbain**. Each company operates with its own branding, templates, invoice numbering, and data isolation through row-level security.

<Callout kind="alert">
  Company management is restricted to users with the **Admin** role.
</Callout>

## Company fields

The following fields are available for each company:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | The legal company name |
| **Address** | Registered business address |
| **VAT number** | Value Added Tax identification number |
| **Logo** | Company logo used in templates and documents |
| **Bank account details** | IBAN and BIC used on invoices and financial documents |

## How company selection affects the system

Company assignment is a foundational setting that influences several areas of ARMS:

| Area | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Templates** | Each company has its own set of document templates for offers, contracts, and invoices |
| **Invoice numbering** | Invoice number sequences run independently per company |
| **Row-level security** | Data is scoped per company, ensuring users only see records belonging to their assigned company |

## Editing a company

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Companies page" icon="building" titleType="p">
    Navigate to **Administration > Companies** in the sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a company" icon="mouse-pointer" titleType="p">
    Click the company you want to edit. The company detail view opens.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Edit" icon="edit" titleType="p">
    Click the **Edit** button to make the company fields editable.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update the desired fields" icon="text-cursor" titleType="p">
    Modify any of the available fields such as address, VAT number, logo, or bank account details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes" icon="save" titleType="p">
    Click **Save** to apply your changes. Click **Cancel** to discard them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Changes to company details such as the logo or bank account are reflected in all future documents generated for that company.
</Callout>
