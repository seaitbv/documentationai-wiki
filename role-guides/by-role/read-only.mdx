## Role overview

The Read-Only role provides **view access** across most ARMS modules. This role is designed for management oversight, reporting, and anyone who needs to monitor operations without making changes.

## Access matrix

| Module | Access Level | What You Can See |
|--------|-------------|------------------|
| Dashboard | Read | KPI widgets, notifications, activity stream |
| Fleet | Read | Trailer overview, details, inspections, km registrations, NP periods |
| Customers | Read | Customer and contact records |
| Offers | Read | Offer overview and details |
| Contracts | Read | Contract overview, details, non-driving days, damage control status |
| Invoicing | Read | Invoice overview and details |
| Planning | Read | Gantt timeline view |
| Templates | -- | Not visible |
| Parameters | -- | Not visible |

<Callout kind="info">
  You cannot create, edit, or delete any records. All forms and action buttons (Create, Edit, Save, Send, Export) are hidden or disabled for your role.
</Callout>

## What you can do

Even with read-only access, you have valuable capabilities:

- **Monitor KPIs** on the Dashboard for a real-time business overview
- **Track open offers** and their statuses across the sales pipeline
- **Review active contracts** and rental periods
- **Check fleet status** including trailer availability, inspections, and non-productive periods
- **View the Planning timeline** to understand fleet utilization
- **Review invoices** and their Exact Online export status
- **Use filters and search** across all accessible modules
- **Click through related records** (e.g., from a contract to its customer, trailer, or invoices)

## Use cases

| Scenario | What to Look At |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Business overview** | Dashboard KPIs: active contracts, available trailers, open offers |
| **Sales pipeline** | Offers module filtered by status; check conversion rate |
| **Fleet utilization** | Planning view; look at rental density per trailer type |
| **Financial overview** | Invoicing module; review monthly invoice totals |
| **Operational health** | Dashboard notifications; check for critical or attention items |

## Navigation tips

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use the Dashboard as your starting point. The KPI widgets are clickable and take you to pre-filtered views, making it quick to drill into specific areas of interest.
</Callout>

- **Dashboard notifications** link directly to the relevant record
- **Planning view** gives a visual overview of all fleet activity
- **Filters on list screens** help you focus on specific companies, statuses, or time periods
- **Breadcrumbs** help you navigate back after drilling into detail screens
