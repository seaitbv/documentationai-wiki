## Core business terms

| English | Dutch (NL) | French (FR) | Definition |
|---------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Trailer | Trailer | Remorque | A STAS tipper or walking floor trailer available for rental |
| Fleet | Vloot | Flotte | The complete set of trailers managed by Atrac or Urbain |
| Customer | Klant | Client | A company that rents trailers |
| Contact | Contact | Contact | A person at a customer company who receives communications |
| Offer | Offerte | Offre | A rental price proposal sent to a customer |
| Contract | Contract | Contrat | A binding rental agreement between entity and customer |
| Invoice | Factuur | Facture | A billing document for rental services |
| Credit note | Creditnota | Note de crédit | A reversal document (e.g., deposit refund) |

## Trailer-specific terms

| English | Dutch (NL) | French (FR) | Definition |
|---------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Plate number | Nummerplaat | Plaque d'immatriculation | Unique license plate — primary identifier for trailers |
| Trailer reference | Trailer referentie | Référence remorque | Internal reference code (e.g., "K28") |
| Display name | Weergavenaam | Nom d'affichage | Concatenation of reference + plate (e.g., "K28 – QAMD180") |
| Nickname | Volgnaam | Surnom | Informal name (e.g., "Delphine", "Sally") |
| Chassis number | Chassisnummer | Numéro de châssis | Unique manufacturer identification |
| Tipper | Kipper | Benne | A trailer type that tips to unload cargo |
| Walking floor | Zelflosser | Fond mouvant | A trailer type with a moving floor for unloading |
| Technical inspection | Technische keuring | Contrôle technique | Mandatory periodic safety inspection |
| Non-productive period | Niet-productieve periode | Période non-productive | Time a trailer is unavailable (maintenance, damage, storage) |
| Km registration | Km-registratie | Enregistrement km | Odometer reading recorded at a point in time |
| Sheet type | Dekzeiltype | Type de bâche | Type of tarpaulin cover on the trailer |
| Volume | Volume | Volume | Cargo capacity in cubic meters (m³) |
| Door type | Deurtype | Type de porte | Style of rear doors on the trailer |

## Status terms

### Trailer statuses

| English | Dutch (NL) | French (FR) | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Active | Actief | Actif | Available for rental |
| Rented | Verhuurd | Loué | Currently under an active contract |
| To check | Te controleren | À vérifier | Returned from rental, pending inspection |
| End-of-life | End-of-life | Fin de vie | No longer available for rental |
| Sold | Verkocht | Vendu | Disposed of through sale |

### Offer statuses

| English | Dutch (NL) | French (FR) | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Created | Aangemaakt | Créé | New offer, not yet sent |
| Sent | Verzonden | Envoyé | Sent to customer via email |
| Accepted | Geaccepteerd | Accepté | Customer accepted the offer |
| Refused | Geweigerd | Refusé | Customer declined the offer |

### Contract statuses

| English | Dutch (NL) | French (FR) | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Created | Aangemaakt | Créé | New contract, not yet sent |
| Sent | Verzonden | Envoyé | Sent to customer for signing |
| Accepted | Geaccepteerd | Accepté | Customer signed the contract |
| In rental | In verhuur | En location | Rental period has started (automatic transition) |
| To check | Te controleren | À vérifier | Trailer returned, pending inspection |
| Completed | Afgerond | Terminé | Contract fully processed and closed |
| Refused | Geweigerd | Refusé | Customer declined the contract |

### Invoice statuses

| English | Dutch (NL) | French (FR) | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Created | Aangemaakt | Créé | Invoice generated, not yet sent |
| Sent | Verzonden | Envoyé | Invoice sent to customer |
| Paid | Betaald | Payé | Payment received |
| Cancelled | Geannuleerd | Annulé | Invoice voided |

## Invoice types

| English | Dutch (NL) | French (FR) | Definition |
|---------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Invoice | Factuur | Facture | Standard rental invoice |
| Advance invoice | Voorschotfactuur | Facture d'acompte | Upfront payment for expected rental |
| Deposit invoice | Waarborgfactuur | Facture de garantie | Security deposit invoice (default 2,000 EUR) |
| Credit note | Creditnota | Note de crédit | Reversal invoice (e.g., deposit return) |

## Billing units

| English | Dutch (NL) | French (FR) | Definition |
|---------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Day | Dag | Jour | Rental charged per calendar day |
| Month | Maand | Mois | Rental charged per calendar month (pro-rata) |
| Km | Km | Km | Rental charged per kilometer driven |

## Financial terms

| English | Dutch (NL) | French (FR) | Definition |
|---------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Deposit | Waarborg | Garantie | Security deposit held during rental (default 2,000 EUR) |
| Advance | Voorschot | Acompte | Upfront payment calculated based on unit type |
| Discount | Korting | Remise | Percentage discount on rental price only (not insurance) |
| VAT | BTW | TVA | Value Added Tax |
| Payment conditions | Betalingscondities | Conditions de paiement | Payment terms (e.g., "30 days net") |
| Non-driving days | Niet-gereden dagen | Jours non-roulés | Days during a day-based contract where the trailer was not used (not billed) |
| Forfait | Forfait | Forfait | Fixed price for 1-day or 2-day rentals |

## System terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **ARMS** | Atrac Rental Management System |
| **RLS** | Row Level Security — PostgreSQL feature enforcing access control at the database level |
| **VIES** | VAT Information Exchange System — EU service for validating VAT numbers |
| **Exact Online** | Accounting software used for invoice processing and payment tracking |
| **Microsoft Graph** | Microsoft API used for creating Outlook email drafts |
| **Server Action** | Next.js feature allowing server-side mutations called from client components |
| **Parameter** | A configurable key-value setting stored in the database |
| **Dropdown value** | A translatable option for select fields, managed centrally |
| **Multi-tenancy** | Supporting multiple legal entities (Atrac, Urbain) in a single application |
| **Entity** | A legal company (Atrac or Urbain) that owns trailers and issues invoices |
